About

How Kubermatic works on 3 simple steps

  • Step 1

    Sign up

    You can create an account in two simple steps by either entering your email and a password or using your Google or Github account.

  • Step 2

    Create a Kubernetes Cluster

    Spin up a new Kubernetes Cluster by choosing your preferred datacenter for your Kubernetes Master.

  • Step 3

    Add nodes to your cluster

    You can scale your Kubernetes Cluster anytime by adding new nodes either on the same datacenter or in a different datacenter than your master.
